World's Population Visualisation

As the years go by the population increases significantly.
This visualisation examines each country's population in relation
to their density, area, and other attributes. First, have
a look at the population of the countries
divided by color.




Now let's take a look at a bubble chart
which represents countries' area and density in relation to
their population, global rank and, growth rate. We can see
that some high density countries like Monaco have relatively a
small land. On the other hand, a country
like Moldova which has a lower density, is the
top country regarding the growth rate in the world.
Moreover, a few interesting bubbles are pointed out below.
