World's Population Visualisation

As the years go by the population increases significantly.
This visualisation examines each country's population in relation
to their density, area, and other attributes. First, have
a look at the population of the countries
divided by color.





Now let's take a look at a bubble chart
which represents countries' Growth Rate and density in relation to
their population and global rank. We can see that
some high density countries like Monaco have relatively a
low growth rate. On the other hand, a country
like Moldova which has a lower density, is the
top country regarding the growth rate in the world.
Moreover, a few interesting bubbles are pointed out below.





Now let's analyze the population of the countries in
1970 regarding their rank, growth rate, and their population
in 2022. The change between population in 1970 to
2020 is observable. However, Countries like China with the
most population in 1970 held their rank in 2020
and small countries like Vatican City stayed the same.
